### Project Description
AN EDUCATIONAL WEBSITE, THAT PROVIDES AWARENESS TO CHILDREN ABOUT THEIR LEGAL RIGHTS AND PERSONAL SPACE.

### The Problem statement
TRADITIONAL CHILD SAFETY EDUCATION FAILS BECAUSE OF BORING DELIVERY,LONG TEXT,WITHOUT VISUAL INTERACTIVITY WHICH OVERWHELMS THE KIDS.

### The Solution
WE ARE SOLVING THIS PROBLEM BY CREATING A WESITE WHICH HELPS THEM UNDERSTAND ABOUT THEIR RIGHTS AND PERSONAL SPACE USING 6 SUPER HEROES.
EACH SUPER HERO HAS THEIR OWN UNIQUE SUPER POWER:
1.CAPATAIN CONSENT:THE RIGHT TO SAY"NO"
2.DETECTIVE DAYA:THE RIGHT TO BE HEARD AND UNDERSTOOD.
3.JUSTICE JAYA:THE RIGHT TO PROTECTION UNDER THE LAW.
4.SIGNAL SURYA:THE RIGHT TO SAFETY.
5.GUARDIAN GOPAL:THE RIGHT TO A SAFE ENVIRONMENT.
6.COUNSELLOR CHITRA:THE RIGHT TO SPEAK UP.
FINALLY THEY ARE PROVIDED WITH SOME SITUATION BASED QUIZ.
